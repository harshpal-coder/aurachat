Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our core vibe. No cap.

1. Zero Data Retention

Omego is built on a "Privacy First" architecture. We do not store your chat logs, your IP address (after your session ends), or any personal identifiers. Once you disconnect, the session data is purged from our temporary memory.

2. Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Tech

By using P2P technology for our chat sessions, your data (text and images) travels directly from your device to the stranger's device. Our servers act only as a "matchmaker" to introduce you, and do not witness the actual conversation.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser local storage to save your user preferences (like Dark/Light theme and your saved interests). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that could be used to identify you across the web.

4. Third-Party Links

If you click on an external link or use a third-party service embedded in our site, their own privacy policy applies. We highkey recommend checking their terms as well.
